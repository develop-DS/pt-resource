Gradski Prevoz Subotica - FAQ

How to open bus route?

From routes tab click on the bus route and you will be presented with bus route timetable.

How to switch bus route direction?

You can switch by clicking at switch button in bus route header.

How to add bus route to favourites?

Add bus route to favourites by clicking on heart icon in bus route screen.

How to change language?

Navigate to settings tab and click language option and pick language.

How to switch dark mode?

Navigate to settings tab and click switch dark mode.
